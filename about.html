The Sakura Bakery History

More than 10+ years experiance

The story of Sakura Bakery traces its roots back to the early 1950s in the heart of Kyoto, Japan. Founded by the visionary baker, Hiroshi Tanaka, Sakura Bakery was a humble establishment nestled amidst the blooming cherry blossom trees. Tanaka's passion for baking and his deep appreciation for the beauty of sakura blossoms inspired him to create a bakery that would embody the essence of this delicate flower.
